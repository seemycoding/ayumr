About Us

AYU Market Research is a leading, full-service global market research/business intelligence and consulting company offering quantitative data collection, qualitative fieldwork, strategic research, desk research and market intelligence solutions. Headquartered in Delhi city, the company has offices worldwide, and provides strategic & consulting services.

AYU Market Research mission is to provide insight across the market, to help the organizations to understand their brand trajectory & measure its progress along the way. We can help you see where you are today, where you are going & help you adjust as part of your path forward to the client to create a trustable and long-lasting relationship with our clients.
